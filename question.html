Wilaw is an online marketplace where in-house teams, businesses and law firms (clients) can find lawyers to work flexibly on a project-by-project basis. Clients post assignments on the marketplace and Wilaw lawyers pitch for that work. Wilaw provides a complete platform to manage the work, invoice and pay.

Join Wilaw as a lawyer member and gain free access to the marketplace. It is only when you transact in the marketplace that you pay a service fee.

As a Wilaw member, you will receive a push notification (email) when an assignment has been posted in your practice area. You can also search the site for assignments that are relevant to you.

On the completion of the assignment, or as agreed with the client, you raise an invoice for the work completed within the Wilaw platform. The invoice is automatically sent to the client. When the invoice is payed, you receive your fee, less the Wilaw service fee appropriate for your membership plan. Our payment gateway is with PromisePay.

The Wilaw marketplace is for commercial lawyers and legal advice, broadly defined. This includes all aspects of Banking and finance Corporate and commercial law Dispute resolution and litigation Employment Government Leasing Mergers and acquisitions Tax The work may require a couple of hours, days or months. There is no upper or lower limit to the amount of time the assignment may take, or the invoice amount. Wilaw is not the right platform for criminal law, family law, residential conveyancing or probate.
